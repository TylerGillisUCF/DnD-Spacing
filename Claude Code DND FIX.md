# Claude Code DND FIX
## Item Replacement Guide for DnD Campaign

**Date:** November 18, 2025
**Purpose:** Replace existing vendor items with 6 new simplified items while maintaining original modifier bonuses

---

## THE 6 NEW ITEMS

### 1. SHIELD TOKEN (3 Coins)
**Effect:** Negates the next curse, penalty, or negative effect you receive
**Usage:** One-time use. Declare when you're about to receive a penalty/curse.
**Simple Rule:** "This didn't happen!" - Cancels one bad thing completely.

**Where to Add:**
- **Potion Seller - TIER 1** (Replace "Basic Potion")
- **Shrine - Copper Bowl Option** (Add as alternative)

---

### 2. FRIEND'S FAVOR (4 Coins)
**Effect:** Save another player from one failed roll by taking the failure yourself
**Reward:** You gain +1 coin AND advantage on your next roll (heroic sacrifice!)
**Usage:** When another player fails a non-combat roll, you can use this to take their failure.
**Simple Rule:** "I'll take that hit for you, friend!" - Helps allies and rewards heroism.

**Where to Add:**
- **Beggar Encounter** (Add as new purchase option after "Help the Beggar")

---

### 3. SECOND CHANCE CHARM (7 Coins)
**Effect:** If you fail a roll by 3 or less, it becomes a success instead
**Usage:** Automatically activates on your next "close call" failure
**Simple Rule:** "Almost counts!" - Turns near-misses into wins.

**Where to Add:**
- **Potion Seller - TIER 2** (Replace "Premium Potion" that costs 8 coins, adjust to 7)
- **Shrine - Silver Bowl Option** (Add as alternative)

---

### 4. KARMA REVERSAL TOKEN (8 Coins)
**Effect:** Turn your next Natural 1 into a Natural 20
**Usage:** Must declare immediately after rolling a Natural 1
**Simple Rule:** "Bad luck becomes AMAZING luck!" - Disaster becomes triumph.

**Where to Add:**
- **Fortune Teller - TIER 2** (Replace "Specific Hint" option at 7 coins, adjust to 8)
- **Merchant/Mystery Option** (Replace Mystery Box)

---

### 5. SHARED BLESSING (10 Coins)
**Effect:** Give another player advantage on any single roll
**Reward for Giver:** Earn +1 coin when they use it successfully
**Usage:** Give to ally, they decide when to use it
**Simple Rule:** "I believe in you!" - Teamwork creates success.

**Where to Add:**
- **Beggar - Generous Donation** (Replace current blessing with this item)
- **Shrine - Golden Bowl** (Add as alternative to current option)

---

### 6. HERO'S DETERMINATION (15 Coins)
**Effect:** Automatically succeed on any single roll of your choice
**Usage:** Declare before rolling. Guarantees success, no roll needed.
**Simple Rule:** "I will NOT fail this!" - Ultimate power when it matters most.

**Where to Add:**
- **Potion Seller - TIER 4** (Replace "Phoenix Tear")
- **Fortune Teller - TIER 3** (Add as alternative to ultimate hint)

---

## ECONOMY BALANCE CHECK

After replacements, coin costs remain balanced:
- **Low tier (3-4 coins):** Shield Token, Friend's Favor
- **Mid tier (7-8 coins):** Second Chance, Karma Reversal
- **High tier (10-15 coins):** Shared Blessing, Hero's Determination

Total economy: 240 coins across 3 players still works
- Each player gets ~60-80 coins total income
- Mandatory expenses: 15 coins (meals)
- Leaves 45-65 coins for strategic purchases
- Can afford 2-3 high-tier items OR several low-tier items
